PRIORITIZING, GOAL SETTING

Goal setting is a powerful process for thinking about your ideal future, and for motivating yourself to turn your vision of this future into reality.

Goals are something that elite athletes, executives, and achievers in all industries do. You gain long-term perspective and immediate drive by setting goals. It helps you focus your learning and better manage your time and resources so that you may enjoy life to the fullest.

Setting precise, well-defined goals allows you to track your progress toward them, take joy in doing so, and make progress in what might have previously looked like a never-ending grind. As you become more aware of your own competency and capacity to accomplish the objectives you have set, you will also gain more self-confidence.


FEATURES OF THIS COURSE


  • Setting Lifelong Objectives Think about the things you wish to accomplish in your lifetime as the first step in defining personal objectives. Setting lifetime objectives helps you gain the comprehensive perspective that informs all other decision-making processes.

  • Setting Smaller Goals After deciding on your long-term objectives, create a five-year plan of shorter objectives that you must accomplish in order to realise your long-term goals.

  • Aim for Smart goals Use of the smart acronym is a helpful strategy for enhancing the impact of goals.

  • Set performance objectives rather than outcome goals. You should be careful to choose goals that you have the most control over. Failure to accomplish a personal goal due to circumstances beyond your control can be quite demoralising.
