CASE STUDY - 3

How Future Leaders See Leadership

How learning and development can help professionals develop leadership skills

Formal learning includes perspective-building activities like facilitated dialogues with high-performing leaders.

Coaching and feedback reinforce qualities like empathy while introducing issues like making hard decisions on profitability.


  • Make certain that your future executives can comprehend all facets of your company, not just their current section. Additionally, you ought to encourage them to have a thorough knowledge of your sector and the potential future prospects for your business. Any aspiring business manager who might one day make choices that influence your company has to know this information.

  • The most effective leadership development occurs when your aspiring leaders are aware of the value of every position within your company. It is crucial that students get information from all viewpoints since one day they will be the ones making decisions that could have an impact on every aspect of the organisation.
