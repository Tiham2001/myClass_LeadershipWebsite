About Us

To unite professionals with a deep interest in the research, practise, and teaching of leadership, the myClass Leadership was established about 30 years ago. The greatest global community devoted to leadership scholarship, development, and practise are connected to us. We achieve our objective through collaborating with our members and partners, who combined have a multiplier effect on leadership and change. The myClass Leadership has brought together great talent from many industries, cultures, disciplines, and generations for twenty years. The greater good of people and communities throughout the world is being shaped by our members and partners, who are in the fore.
