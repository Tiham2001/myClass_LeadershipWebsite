DELEGATION EMPOWERMENT

Increasing status, power, and control through delegation. A distributed control system has a higher likelihood of surviving than a centralised control system.

What type of a leader are you in terms of delegation and empowerment? Do you assign assignments to your team, or do you inspire and provide them the tools they need to complete the work independently? Does your team still require delegation or is it independent enough to be certified?

When it comes to leadership, delegation and empowerment are powerful concepts. When a manager delegated, they were asking their team members to follow official instructions but also using some of their own initiative. When a leader empowers their team, they allow them the freedom to decide how they want to carry out a particular task.
